LCD Technology: Liquid Crystals

Most screens use LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) technology. Here's how it works:

ğŸ§ª The LCD Stack (from back to front):

  1. Backlight - bright white LEDs that provide the light
  2. Polarizer - filters light waves to one direction
  3. Liquid Crystals - molecules that twist when electricity is applied
  4. Color Filters - red, green, and blue filters for each sub-pixel
  5. Second Polarizer - blocks or allows light through based on crystal twist

The Magic: When you apply voltage to liquid crystals, they twist and change how much light passes through. More voltage = more twist = darker pixel. Less voltage = less twist = brighter pixel!
